Role Definitions & Access Levels

Admin

Neuroscience Director, Chair, Research Director

  • Full database access
  • Approve/deny access requests
  • Manage all users & roles
  • View audit log
  • All project, grant, IRB data

Faculty

Neurology & Neurosurgery Faculty

  • Own projects & grants
  • View enrolled patients/subjects
  • Publications & presentations
  • Submit forum requests
  • Schedule meetings

CRC

Clinical Research Coordinator

  • Patient enrollment data
  • Data collection tools
  • IRB regulatory documents
  • Schedule coordination
  • Visit tracking

Research Nurse

Clinical Research Nurse

  • Patient visit procedures
  • Adverse event reporting
  • Study protocol access
  • Enrollment updates

Resident

Neurology/Neurosurgery Residents

  • Assigned project access
  • Educational resources
  • Publication tracking
  • View-only for most data

Medical Student

Rotating Medical Students

  • Assigned project access only
  • Educational resources
  • Performance evaluations
  • Most restricted access

Research Fellow

Postdoctoral & Clinical Fellows

  • Assigned projects & grants
  • Publications access
  • Data & resources
  • Forum requests

Budget & Contracts

Financial & Contract Staff

  • Budget tracking per project
  • Contract management
  • Grant financial data
  • No clinical data access

Statistician

Biostatistician / Data Analyst

  • Forum consultation requests
  • Dataset access
  • Analysis tools & resources
  • Statistical consultation

APP / NP / PA

Advanced Practice Providers

  • View assigned projects
  • Data collection access
  • Educational resources
  • CME tracking

RN

Research / Clinical Nurses

  • Patient visit procedures
  • Adverse event reporting
  • Study protocol access
  • CITI training required

IRB Reviewer

IRB Review & Compliance

  • Approve/deny IRB protocols
  • Upload approval documents
  • Assign protocol numbers
